Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our credit cards

How do I apply for a credit card?
Simply click on the "Apply Now" button next to your preferred card, fill out the online application form, and submit the required documents. Our team will review your application within 24-48 hours.
What documents are required?
You'll need a government-issued ID, proof of address, income proof (salary slips or bank statements for the last 3 months), and PAN card.
Is there an annual fee?
Our Classic card has zero annual fee. The Gold and Platinum cards have an annual fee which can be waived on meeting minimum spending criteria.
How can I track my reward points?
You can easily track your reward points through our mobile app or by logging into your online banking account. Points are updated in real-time after each transaction.
What if my card is lost or stolen?
Call our 24/7 helpline immediately to block your card. We'll issue a replacement card within 3-5 business days at no extra cost.
